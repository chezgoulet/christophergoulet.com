02 — COMMUNITY SCOUTING

The 161st Catamounts

Founder & Chief Tiguidou

161st Catamounts

Founder and Scoutmaster of the 161st Catamounts, a community scouting troop chartered under Outdoor Service Guides (OSG). All-adult, community defense-oriented, democratic governance — explicitly not a militia. Core training in de-escalation, wilderness first response, emergency communications, and community resilience. Based in Windsor, operating throughout the Upper Connecticut River Valley.

"Find your path, change the world!"

Purpose

Community Defense & Resilience

The 161st Catamounts is an all-adult community scouting troop focused on building resilience from the ground up. Democratic governance, wilderness skills, emergency preparedness, and de-escalation training — everything a community needs to take care of itself. We are not a militia. We are neighbors learning to be useful in a crisis.
