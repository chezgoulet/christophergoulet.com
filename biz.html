02 / PROOF

I don't just advise this. I lived it.

I'VE DONE THIS MYSELF

I turned my family's cookie bakery into a worker-owned business — and tripled its revenue.

"There's actually a lot of shared DNA between baking cookies and shipping software. Both take source code, put it through a pipeline, package it, and deploy it."

Covered Bridge is a thirty-year-old family cookie bakery in Vermont. Four honest recipes, real ingredients, no words you can't pronounce. We converted it to 100% worker ownership — four owners, a living wage, and good working conditions — and kept a thirty-year legacy in the hands of the people who actually do the work.

It's hard. Collaborating with people as equals is hard. It's worth it. But it's not a switch you can flip — it's an active engagement you choose every day, just like any relationship.
