Who I am

Not just a builder — a steward

I'm a systems architect, infrastructure builder, and community organizer from Windsor, Vermont — relocating to Sherbrooke, Québec. I've spent my career at the intersection of technology and human systems: everything from designing distributed inference clusters to running for local office to building cooperative automation pipelines.

I believe the best technology is the kind you can own — no dependencies, no phone-home, no rent-seeking. The House (my multi-agent system) and Phonon (my private inference cluster) are expressions of that philosophy: infrastructure that serves its operator, not the other way around.

When I'm not building, I'm playing music, researching Québec sovereignty, learning Inuktitut, or planning my next move in the great Vermont-to-Québec pipeline.
